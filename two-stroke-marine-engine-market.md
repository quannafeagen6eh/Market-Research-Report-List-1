<p><p>A two-stroke marine engine is an internal combustion engine that completes a power cycle in two strokes of the piston, utilizing both the upward and downward movements to achieve combustion. Such engines are primarily used in larger vessels, including cargo ships and tankers, due to their efficiency and reliability.</p><p>The Two-stroke Marine Engine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.4% during the forecast period.
