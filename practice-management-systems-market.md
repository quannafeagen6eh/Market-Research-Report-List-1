<p><p>Practice Management Systems (PMS) are essential tools for healthcare organizations, categorized into two main types: Integrated and Standalone systems. Integrated Practice Management Systems combine multiple functions, such as billing, scheduling, and electronic health records, into a single platform, enhancing workflow efficiency and data coherence.
